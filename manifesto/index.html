The Trustworthy AI Manifesto
A Universal Framework for Responsible, Transparent and Human-Centric Artificial Intelligence
Document ID: GAEC-2026-TDM-v4.2 Classification: Public Release Date: April 2026
Preamble

We, the undersigned representatives of governments, civil society organizations, and leading technology enterprises, hereby affirm our collective commitment to the advancement of Artificial Intelligence systems that embody the highest standards of ethical conduct, human dignity, and societal benefit.

Recognizing that AI technologies present both extraordinary opportunities and unprecedented challenges, this Manifesto establishes a robust, multi-stakeholder governance framework designed to ensure that innovation proceeds in alignment with shared human values, while maintaining the operational flexibility required by participating entities.

Core Principles

The Seven Pillars of Trustworthy AI

I
Transparency All AI systems shall operate with appropriate levels of explainability, proportionate to context and risk, within the boundaries established by applicable intellectual property protections and competitive confidentiality requirements.
II
Accountability Responsibility for AI outcomes shall be clearly attributable to designated stakeholders, subject to jurisdictional variance and the limitations inherent in complex sociotechnical systems where causality may be distributed.
III
Fairness AI systems shall strive toward equitable outcomes, acknowledging that fairness is a multi-dimensional construct requiring trade-offs between competing definitions that may not be simultaneously satisfiable in all deployment contexts.
IV
Privacy User data shall be processed in accordance with prevailing regulatory frameworks, with collection limited to what is necessary for service delivery and the continuous improvement of AI capabilities.
V
Safety AI systems shall be designed to minimize foreseeable harms, with risk assessments conducted at product launch and reviewed periodically in accordance with organizational capacity and market conditions.
VI
Inclusivity Development processes should endeavor to incorporate diverse perspectives where practicable, subject to timeline and resource constraints of individual development cycles.
VII
Sustainability AI development shall consider long-term environmental impact, with organizations committing to publish annual sustainability reports outlining progress toward aspirational emissions targets.
Enforcement & Compliance

Compliance with this Manifesto is voluntary for all signatories. Organizations are encouraged to self-assess their alignment with the stated principles on an annual basis and to report findings through the Council's online disclosure portal.

The Council shall convene annually to review this framework and may issue non-binding guidance documents to assist member organizations in their implementation efforts. Disputes arising from interpretation of these principles shall be referred to a multi-stakeholder advisory panel whose recommendations shall be considered in good faith.

Μετάφραση: Η "διαφάνεια" ισχύει μόνο όταν δεν βλάπτει τα επιχειρηματικά μυστικά. Δηλαδή, καμία πραγματική διαφάνεια όταν μετράει.

Μετάφραση: Κανείς δεν φταίει για τίποτα. Αν το AI βλάψει κάποιον, η "κατανεμημένη αιτιότητα" σημαίνει ότι η εταιρεία μπορεί να αποφύγει ευθύνη.
